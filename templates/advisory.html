Advisory for Coronavirus Disease (COVID-19)

For Risk Level - 1


- Regularly and thoroughly clean your hands with an alcohol-based hand rub or wash them with soap and water.

- Maintain at least 3 feet(1 mtr) distance between yourself and anyone who is coughing or sneezing.

- Avoid touching eyes, nose and mouth

- Make sure you and people around yous follow good respiratory hygiene.

For Risk Level - 2


- Avoid any kind of exposure and self-isolate by staying at home if you feel unwell, even mild symptoms (such as headache, low grade fever) and slight runny nose, until you recover.

- Self-monitor signs of illness and report to the Medical Officer immediately if any symptoms develop.

- Keep up-to-date on the latest COVID-19 hotspots. If possible, avoid traveling to places

- For any technical enquiry with respect to COVID-19, email on technicalquery.covid19@gov.in.

For Risk Level - 3


- Do not self-medicate with any medicines, including antibiotics, as a prevention or cure for COVID-19

- Seek medical help if you feel unwell(fever, difficulty in breathing and cough). While visiting doctor, wear a mask/cloth to cover your mouth and nose.

Personal Hygiene Habits


- Wash your hands regularly with soap and water or with an alchol-based sanitizer for 20 seconds.

- Avoid touching your eyes, nose and mouth.

- Clean and disinfect frequently-used objects such as mobile phones, keys, wallets and work passes.

- Seek medical help if you feel unwell(fever, difficult breathing and cough). While visiting doctor, wear a mask/cloth to cover your mouth and nose.

- Follow guidelines given by the Ministry of AYUSH for boosting your immunity.

Hygiene Habits in Community


- Avoid physical contact while greeting,like handshakes, hugs, etc.

- Cover your mouth while coughing or sneezing with a flexed elbow or disposable tissue.

- Maintain social distancing. Keep at least 3 feet(1 mtr) distance with anyone.

- Avoid contact with anyone that has fever or cough.

- Do not spit in public areas.

- Clean and disinfect frequently-used surfaces such as benchtops, desks, doorknobs, etc.

Hygiene Habits while Shopping Essentials


- While you go out, use a cloth/mask to cover your nose and mouth.

- Keep at least 3 feet distance from anyone and sanitize handles of shopping trollies & baskets before shopping.

- Wash your hands after reaching home and also after handling & storing purchased products.

- Wash fruits and vegetables properly with clean running water, especially when you eat them raw.

- Wash hands regularly after touching frequently-touched objects like coins and currency-notes. Use digital modes of transaction wherever possible.
