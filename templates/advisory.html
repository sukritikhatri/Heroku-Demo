Advisory for CoronaVirus Disease (COVID-19)

For Risk Level - 1


- Clean your hands regularly & thoroughly with an alcohol-based hand rub or wash them with soap & water.

- Maintain at least 3 feet(1 mtr) distance from anyone who is coughing or sneezing.

- Avoid touching your eyes, nose and mouth.

- Make sure you and your closed ones are following proper hygiene.

For Risk Level - 2


- Isolate yourself at home if you are feeling unwell or facing even mild symptoms like headache or low-grade fever. Stay indoors until you recover.

- Self-monitor any signs of illness and report to the Medical Officer immediately, if any symptom develops.

- Keep yourself updated regarding the latest COVID-19 hotspots. Kindly avoid travelling to those places.

- For any technical query with respect to COVID-19, you may email on "technicalquery.covid19@gov.in".

For Risk Level - 3


- Avoid taking medicines without any doctor's prescription, as a prevention or cure for COVID-19.

- Seek medical help if you are feeling unwell. While visiting the doctor, wear a mask/cloth to cover your mouth & nose.

- Call State helpline number or Ministry of Health and Family Welfareâ€™s 24x7 helpline at 011-23978046 or 1075(Toll Free) and follow the instructions.

- List of Labs (Govt. & Private) can be accessed at: "icmr.nic.in".

Personal Hygiene Habits


- Wash your hands regularly with soap & water or with an alchol-based sanitizer for 20 seconds.

- Avoid touching your eyes, nose and mouth.

- Clean and disinfect frequently-used objects such as mobile phones, keys, wallets and work passes.

- Seek medical help if you feel unwell(fever, difficulty in breathing, cough, etc.). While visiting the doctor, wear a mask/cloth to cover your mouth & nose.

- Follow guidelines given by the Ministry of AYUSH for boosting your immunity.

Hygiene Habits in Community


- Avoid physical contact with others like handshakes, hugs, etc.

- Cover your mouth while coughing or sneezing with a flexed elbow or a disposable tissue.

- Maintain social distancing. Keep at least 3 feet(1 mtr) distance with anyone.

- Avoid contact with anyone who is having fever or cough.

- Do not spit in public places.

- Clean and disinfect frequently-used surfaces such as benchtops, desks, doorknobs, etc.

Hygiene Habits while Shopping Essentials


- While going out, use a cloth/mask to cover your nose & mouth.

- Keep at least 3 feet(1 mtr) distance from anyone and sanitize the handles of baskets & trollies before shopping.

- Wash your hands immediately after reaching home and also, after handling & storing purchased products.

- Wash fruits and vegetables properly with clean running-water, especially before you eat them raw.

- Wash your hands regularly after touching frequently-touched objects like coins and currency-notes. Wherever possible, use digital modes of transaction.
