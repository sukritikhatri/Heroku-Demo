India Fights Against Corona

Advisory for Coronavirus Disease (COVID-19)

Personal Hygiene Habits


- Wash your hands regularly with soap and water or with an alchol-based sanitizer for 20 seconds.

- Avoid touching your eyes, nose and mouth.

- Clean and disinfect frequently used objects such as mobile phones, keys, wallets and work passes.

- Seek medical help if you feel unwell(fever, difficult breathing and cough). While visiting doctor wear a mask/cloth to cover your mouth and nose.

- Follow guidelines given by the Ministry of AYUSH for boosting your immunity.

Hygiene Habits in Community


- Avoid physical contact while greeting,like handshakes etc.

- Cover your mouth while coughing or sneezing with a flexed elbow or disposable tissue.

- Maintain social distancing. Keep at least 3 feet(1 mtr) distance with anyone.

- Avoid contact with anyone that has a fever and cough.

- Do not spit in public areas.

- Clean and disinfect frequently used surfaces such as benchtops, desks and doorknobs.

Hygiene Habits while Shopping Essentials


- Use a cloth mask to cover your nose and mouth while you go out.

- Keep at least 3 feet distance from other, sanitize handles of shopping trollies and baskets before shopping.

- Wash your hands once you reach home and also after handling and storing purchased products.

- Wash fruits and vegetables thoroughly with clean running water, especially we you eat them raw.

- Also wash your hands regularly after touching frequently-touched objects like coins and banknotes. Use digital transactions as much as you can.
